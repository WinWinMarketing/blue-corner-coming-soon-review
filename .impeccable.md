## Design Context

### Users
Blue Corner serves Canadian men who may be carrying burnout, isolation, anxiety, addiction, or crisis without naming it, plus therapists and founding partners who want to support them. The coming-soon experience must make guarded visitors feel recognized without exposing, diagnosing, or speaking down to them, then provide a calm route to early access.

### Brand Personality
Protective, direct, and quietly defiant. Blue Corner should feel like an editorial public-interest campaign with the steadiness of someone waiting between rounds: human, plain-spoken, credible, and never performatively clinical.

### Aesthetic Direction
Use the supplied reference as the master visual system: off-white editorial pages, dominant bright Blue Corner blue, dark navy ink, rare yellow signal bars, hard rules, oversized left-anchored typography, numbered evidence, asymmetric compositions, and intact approved corner assets. The master uses a clean 3:2 empty-ring photograph with a DOM-rendered blue corner glyph; no interface text or controls are baked into raster art. Boxing should appear through the metaphor of the corner, rope geometry, bell rhythm, canvas, tape, and held space—not fighters, sports-poster drama, games, glossy stock scenes, decorative gradients, or logo abuse.

### Design Principles
- Build twelve distinct editorial narratives from one unmistakable Blue Corner system.
- Keep route 01 as the add-on-free master; routes 02–12 each receive exactly one scoped enhancement and no other core-page drift.
- Preserve the reference page’s section rhythm, density, hierarchy, and sharp geometry.
- At 1440px, use 56px gutters, the measured 43/51 hero, 84%-wide four-column evidence grid, 36/64 symptom split, 12.6% manifesto rail, five-column roadmap, and 39/56 conversion split.
- Keep the locked line breaks in every core display heading and keep core content visible without scroll-triggered reveal.
- Use bright blue as the primary field; reserve navy for ink, text, and limited manifesto surfaces.
- Keep yellow rare and functional: dividers, numbers, active states, and primary action emphasis.
- Make every claim sourceable, every form state explicit, every control keyboard-accessible, and every layout stable from 320px through desktop.
