It doesn't always look like what you'd expect

It looks like this.

01

No drive. No sleep.

Waking up already tired of the day. Burnout that nobody clocks, carried quietly because saying it out loud still feels like losing.

02

Numbing out to get through it.

The drink. The doomscroll. The porn. The bet. Anything that turns the volume down for an hour.

03

Focus gone. Brain rot.

Work slipping. Can't lock in. Walking into a room and forgetting why you're standing there.

04

The people closest to him paying for it.

Distance from his partner and his kids. The heaviest thing he carries, and the one he'll never name.

What Blue Corner is

Not a clinic. A corner.

In boxing, the underdog fights out of the blue corner. It's where his people wait between rounds, patch him up, and tell him he's still in it. That's what this is. Real people who've been there, in your corner for the guy who never asks. You're not broken. You've just been fighting without a corner.
